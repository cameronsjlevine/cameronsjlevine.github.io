Cameron Levine

A game development portfolio.

Although I primarily see myself as a programmer, I also have a passion for and experience in graphic design. Most of this work was made in Adobe Illustrator, and some of it was made in order to enhance my own video game projects.

Brushstroke

  • Brushstroke is a 3D dungeon-crawling RPG
  • All the visual assets and UI elements are original, made by me in Adobe Illustrator

CL Lettermark

  • A lettermark of my initials, CL
  • The shape is meant to flow smoothly from one end to the other, with the contrasting colors clearly indicating both letters
  • Made with Adobe Illustrator

Craigslist Website Redesign Mockup

  • A mockup of a redesign of the Craigslist website
  • The primary goal was to professionalize the website, so that it looked more modern and user-friendly
  • Made with Adobe Illustrator, Adobe XD

Burgers Ahoy! Splash Screen Mockup

  • A mockup of a splash screen for the fictional burger restaurant "Burgers Ahoy!"
  • The idea with the branding was to be neutral; that is, to be simple and clean in order to appeal to any audience
  • Made with Adobe Illustrator, Adobe XD

2023 Typographic Calendar

  • A 2023 calendar with a typographic theme named "The Sands of Time"
  • The primary focus was not only to be visually interesting with the layout and illustrations, but to also create a design that allowed for consistency throughout an entire calendar
  • Made with Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign
